Watermelon Feta Salad

A refreshing and delicious summer salad! Perfect for those weeknights where you just can't be bothered, but your kids are still bugging you for food!

Ingredients

  • ½ large chilled seedless watermelon, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 cup thinly sliced fresh basil leaves
  • ½ cup minced fresh mint leaves
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 1 (4 ounce) package crumbled feta cheese
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, or more to taste
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
